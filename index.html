Welcome to Tower of Hanoi
Object of the game is to move all the disks over to Tower target.
But you cannot place a larger disk onto a smaller disk.
Instructions
Move disk: To move the disks simply use the buttons under the towers
  1. >: Move the disk to the adjacent tower on right side
  2. >>: Move the disk to farthest tower on right side
  3. <: Move the disk to the adjacent tower on left side
  4. <<: Move the disk to farthest tower on left side

Solve: To show user how to move disks step by step
  1. Solve quickly: The game solves the puzzle quickly
  2. Solve normally: The game solves the puzzle at normal speed
  3. Solve slowly: The game solves the puzzle slowly
